About SA Design Services

SA Design Services Ltd is a professional architectural design practice based in London, specializing in residential and small commercial projects. Founded and led by Director Surya Adhikari, our team brings together years of experience in architectural design and planning.

We pride ourselves on delivering high-quality, innovative design solutions tailored to our clients' specific needs and budgets. Our approach combines creative vision with practical considerations to produce designs that are both beautiful and functional.

Whether you're planning an extension, conversion, or new build, we guide you through every step of the process from initial concept through to completion, ensuring your project meets all regulatory requirements while realizing your vision.
